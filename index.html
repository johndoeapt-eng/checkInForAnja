Television Settings

You have two remotes for the TV setup:

  • Left Remote: Controls the TV and displays various German and English channels.
  • Right Remote: Controls the receiver for Croatian and Slovenian channels.

How to switch to Croatian & Slovenian channels:

  1. Press the button on the left remote that has a red box around it.
  2. Choose 'hdmi2' as the connection type.
  3. Turn on the receiver using the right remote.
